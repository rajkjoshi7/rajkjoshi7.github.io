About Me

I work on computational modeling of astrophysical flows, specifically focusing on simulating the behavior of matter in the vicinity of compact objects like black holes and neutron stars. My research involves advanced numerical methods and simulations to understand accretion processes and relativistic jets.

My Research

My work focuses on the following areas:

  • Numerical simulations of accretion disks and relativistic flows around black holes.
  • Studying the formation and dynamics of relativistic jets.
  • Studying the interiors of Neutron stars.
  • Development of new algorithms and simulation codes for astrophysical simulations.
